digital color pattern recorder

whichlight

Instructions:

Click a link to open up the respective pattern. When you press the spacebar it will start recording, when you press it again it will stop recording.

The video encoding will take some time. You can open up the developer console to get some realtime feedback. View> Developer> Javascript Console

When you stop recording, the video is encoded and afterwards automatically saved to your downloads folder as a webm

You can zoom in or out with the browser controls, the dimensions of the animations are 1920x1080

Patterns

twoism : click and move in vertical direction to change thickness of circles.

triangle

bolt : click and move mouse vertically to change orientation of the bolts.

dots : click and move mouse vertically to change size of dots

kelp : click to change color, move vertically to change speed, and horizontally to change size of boxes. It's really fun to make it fast and the suddenly slow everything down.

bamboo : click to change colors

mist : move vertically to change size of diamonds. When made big again they will flash synchronously. Refresh to make it more random again.

rays : move horizontally to change direction of rays, click to make them sharp again.

stars : click to change colors.
